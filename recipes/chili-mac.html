Healthy Chili Mac

Description

This Healthy Chili Mac is the perfect pairing of two of my favorite meals: chili and mac and cheese.

This healthy meal is perfectly portioned for the 21 Day Fix, and gluten-free, too! This is a healthy meal for the whole family.

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 1 lb ground lean turkey or lean ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 bell peppers, diced
  • 1 small zucchini, cut into quarters
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of kidney beans, drained and rinsed well
  • 1 1/2 cups of uncooked gluten free elbows
  • 2 teaspoons of chili powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 (14oz) can of tomato sauce
  • 1 (14oz) can of mild diced tomatoes with green chilis
  • 2/3 - 1 1/3 cup of shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to the directions on the package.
  2. In a large pot or dutch oven, heat olive oil over medium heat and then saute the ground turkey or beef until nearly cooked through.
  3. Add in onions, bell peppers, and saute for a couple minutes until fragrant and veggies begin to soften. Then add in garlic and zucchini, cooking for an additional minute.
  4. Mix seasonings and salt into tomato sauce. Add sauce to veggie mixture, along with the beans and diced tomatoes. Cook for 10 minutes to let flavors meld together and the liquid to reduce.
  5. Finally, stir in pasta and cheese. Garnish with cilantro and enjoy!
